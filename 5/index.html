Part A.0: Setup & Text Prompts

I ran the model using a random seed value of 180 and tried out several different combinations for num_inference_steps using 20 and 200. In all cases, the quality of the outputs were decent but not impressive. Meaning, it was somewhat obvious that they were AI-generated as they didn't look very realistic (especially due to the colors), and they didn't match what I had in mind but they weren't terrible either. At num_inference_steps = 20, the outputs looked very smooth and didn't have much texture / detail. Meanwhile, at a value of 200, the outputs had much more detail but the colors still looked somewhat exagerrated, especially for the pasta prompt. For the airport prompt, I think it could have done a better job making it clear that it was an airport in Japan specifically. And for the "man in love" prompt, I think it wasn't super obvious that the person was in love (except for maybe the first output) -- it seemed like a normal glance and the expressions didn't look soft. I also tried a num_inference_step value of 400 and interestingly, there were visible small circles in the outputs.
